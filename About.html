ABOUT ME.

Hi there! I'm Ethan Jed V. Carbonell, born on January 9, 2005. I'm passionate about expressing myself through art, and drawing is my primary outlet for creativity. Whether it's sketching, painting, or experimenting with different mediums, I find joy in bringing my imagination to life on paper.

Music is a significant part of my life, and you'll often find me with headphones on, lost in the melodies and rhythms that inspire me. From soothing tunes to upbeat tracks, my playlist is diverse

When it comes to entertainment, I'm an avid film enthusiast. Whether it's exploring the intricacies of storytelling, analyzing cinematography, or simply enjoying a good movie, I appreciate the power of cinema to evoke emotions and provoke thought.

I see life as a canvas waiting to be filled with experiences, creativity, and meaningful connections. As I continue my journey, I look forward to discovering more about myself and the world around me, one drawing, song, and film at a time.
